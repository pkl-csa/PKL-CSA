About Us

What is PKL?

PKL or Philippine Karatedo League Inc. is a Karate organization that is composed of different styles and was formed many years ago by PKL Shihan Dennis Q. Aquino, the founder of PKL. It is one of the largest Karate organizations of the country with thousands of members. PKL also have branches outside the country (international) and continuing the advocate through Karate sport.

History of Karatedo

Karate's evolution took place over several centuries in East Asia, gradually developing and becoming systematized in Okinawa during the 17th century. This systematization likely occurred among individuals who were prohibited from carrying weapons, leading them to develop effective unarmed combat techniques for self-defense and protection.

In the 1920s, karate was introduced to Japan, marking a significant milestone in its history. The Japanese were intrigued by the art's practicality and philosophical aspects, which led to its integration into the country's martial arts culture.

As karate spread and gained popularity, different schools and systems emerged, each emphasizing slightly different techniques and training methods. These variations gave rise to various karate styles, such as Shotokan, Goju-Ryu, Wado-Ryu, and Shito-Ryu, among others. Each style is characterized by its unique approaches to striking, blocking, and footwork, as well as its specific forms (kata) and sparring techniques (kumite).

Throughout its development and dissemination, karate's emphasis on discipline, respect, and self-improvement appealed to practitioners beyond Japan's borders. It gradually gained recognition internationally, with dedicated practitioners and masters sharing their knowledge and skills with the rest of the world.

Welcome to PKL-CSA: Empowering Minds and Bodies Through Martial Arts Excellence

At the Philippine Karatedo Club - Cavite Shitokai Academy, we are dedicated to fostering the art of karate while nurturing the physical and mental growth of our members. With a rich history and a commitment to traditional values, we offer a holistic approach to martial arts training that goes beyond physical techniques.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to discover the transformative power of karate. Through rigorous training, mentorship, and a supportive community, we aim to instill discipline, confidence, and respect in our members, helping them excel not only in the dojo but also in their daily lives.


What Sets Us Apart

1. Expert Instruction: Our club is led by a team of experienced and highly skilled instructors who are deeply rooted in the principles of karate. They guide students through progressive training programs, ensuring that beginners and advanced practitioners alike can learn and grow effectively.


2. Traditional Values: We uphold the traditional values of karate, including respect for oneself and others, humility, and perseverance. These values are woven into every aspect of our training, promoting personal growth and character development.


3. Holistic Approach: Beyond physical training, we prioritize mental and emotional well-being. Our classes include mindfulness exercises and discussions on the philosophical aspects of martial arts, providing a comprehensive learning experience.


4. Inclusive Community: Our club is a diverse and inclusive community that welcomes individuals from all walks of life. We believe that unity in diversity enriches the learning environment and creates lasting friendships.


5. Competitive Excellence: For those interested in pushing their limits, we offer avenues for competitive karate. Our club has a proven track record of producing skilled competitors who excel in local, national, and international tournaments.
