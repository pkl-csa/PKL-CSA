What is PKL?

PKL or Philippine Karatedo League Inc. is a Karate organization that is composed of different styles and was formed many years ago by PKL Shihan Dennis Q. Aquino, the founder of PKL. It is one of the largest Karate organizations of the country with thousands of members. PKL also have branches outside the country (international) and continuing the advocate through Karate sport.
